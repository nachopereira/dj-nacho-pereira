// ABOUT ME

Forged in the neon-drenched back alleys of the digital metropolis, Nacho Pereira is a sonic architect of the cyberpunk era. My sets are a journey through dystopian soundscapes, blending hard-hitting dark techno with futuristic, glitched-out melodies. I craft experiences that blur the line between human and machine, inviting you to lose yourself in the rhythm of the future.

// FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What kind of music do you play?

I specialize in cyberpunk and dark techno, blending hard-hitting beats with futuristic, glitched-out melodies. My sets are designed to take you on a journey through dystopian soundscapes.

Are you available for private events?

Yes, I am available for private events, club nights, and festivals. Please use the booking form in the "Booking" section to send me your event details, and I'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Do you offer custom mixes or productions?

Currently, my focus is on live performances and curated sets. However, I'm always open to discussing unique collaboration opportunities. Feel free to reach out via the contact form with your ideas.
